Floor plan

The images below show the new (left) and existing (right) house floor plans. A majority of our work was focused on opening up the front of the house. In the existing floor plan, the master bed room and closet were located directly to the left of the front door. These walls were removed to open up the front of the house. The existing master bedroom now serves as a study/3rd bedroom. To keep with the open floor plan, we also moved the kitchen from the back corner of the house to the existing dining room. The existing kitchen will then be converted to a 2nd bedroom. All in all, this setup divides the house into public space in the front and private space in the back.

In the process of converting rooms and removing walls we also moved a number of windows. The existing dinning room windows were moved to the new dinning room to allow for kitchen counter tops and a new smaller casement window was installed in their place. The existing master bedroom window was then doubled up using the old south window. In addition, the small casement window located in the existing closet was moved to the front of the house to create a group of 3 casement windows. These windows were shifted slightly to align with the center of the new living room.
